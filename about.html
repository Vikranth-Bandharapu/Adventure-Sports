Our Story

Born From a Love of the Wild

WildPeak Adventures was founded in 2018 in the adventure capital of India — Rishikesh — by a passionate group of mountaineers, naturalists, and storytellers who believed that the mountains had more to teach us than any classroom ever could.

What started as a small team of 5 certified guides leading weekend treks has evolved into one of India's most trusted adventure tour operators. Today, we operate across 8 Indian states, offering more than 50 meticulously crafted routes through the Himalayas, Western Ghats, Northeast India, and beyond.

Our mission is simple: to create transformative experiences that push personal limits, forge deep connections with nature, and leave our adventurers forever changed. Every expedition we run is built on three pillars — absolute safety, environmental respect, and unforgettable moments.

With 500+ successful expeditions completed and over 10,000 adventurers who have trusted us with their wildest dreams, we remain as committed as ever to the mountains that inspired us to begin.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

Every adventure we curate is guided by these four principles that define who we are and what we stand for.

Safety First

Every expedition is planned with rigorous risk assessments, certified guides, and industry-leading emergency protocols. Your safety is never negotiable.

Sustainability

We operate with a zero-waste philosophy, support local communities, and actively participate in trail conservation efforts to preserve India's natural heritage.

Excellence

From hand-picked accommodations to gourmet trail meals, we obsess over every detail so that your experience exceeds every expectation, every time.

Community

We build bonds — between adventurers, with local guides, and within the villages we visit. Every journey strengthens the broader WildPeak community.
