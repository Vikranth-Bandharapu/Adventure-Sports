FAQs

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best time to visit the Himalayas?

The best time is May–June and September–October when skies are clear and trails are accessible. Winter treks (Dec–Feb) are also beautiful but require more preparation and cold-weather gear.

Are the trips suitable for beginners?

Absolutely! We have packages for every level — from easy day hikes and relaxed camping to extreme high-altitude climbs. Our team will suggest the right adventure based on your fitness and experience level.

What should I pack for a trek?

We provide a detailed packing list for each trip after booking. Generally: layered clothing, trekking shoes, rain gear, sunscreen, water bottle, snacks and a first aid kit. All technical gear is provided by us.

Do you offer group discounts?

Yes! Groups of 8 or more get 10–25% off depending on group size, destination and season. Corporate groups and educational institutions get additional benefits including customized itineraries.

What is your cancellation policy?

Full refund if cancelled 30+ days before departure. 50% refund for 15–29 days. No refund for cancellations under 14 days. In case of weather/emergency, we offer full rescheduling at no extra cost.

Is travel insurance included?

Basic adventure travel insurance is included in all packages covering emergency evacuation and medical expenses. We recommend purchasing additional personal travel insurance for extra peace of mind.

How do I book a custom itinerary?

Fill the contact form on this page with your preferred dates, destination, group size and any special requirements. Our experts will send you a custom proposal within 4 hours during business hours.

What safety measures do you follow?

All guides are WFA/NIMAS certified. We carry satellite phones, GPS beacons, oxygen cylinders and complete medical kits on every expedition. Pre-trip acclimatization and daily safety briefings are mandatory for all participants.
